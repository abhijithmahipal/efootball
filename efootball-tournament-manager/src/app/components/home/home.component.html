Welcome to eFootball Tournament Manager

Organize and manage your eFootball tournaments with ease. Create round-robin leagues, track match results, and follow the standings in real-time.

Tournament Features

Player Management

Add and manage tournament participants with ease. Minimum 5 players required for tournament generation.

Automatic Scheduling

Generate complete round-robin schedules automatically. Every player plays every other player twice.

Live Standings

Track points, wins, losses, and goal differences in real-time as match results are entered.

Playoff System

Automatic playoff generation for top 4 players with semifinals, final, and third-place matches.

Tournament Administration

Are you organizing a tournament? Access the admin panel to manage players, generate schedules, and enter match results.
